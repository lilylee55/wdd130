Overview

Purpose

The business intent is to provide a thrilling and safe white water rafting experience to individuals and groups for a fee.

Audience

People who is interested in thrilling rafting experience

  • Who are the target customers? Describe their life (or business) situation (age, financial situation, interests, and so on)

    Age: Primarily 18-50 years old. This age range is likely to be physically fit and adventurous enough to participate in white-water rafting.

    Financial Situation: Middle to upper-middle class who can afford adventure-based vacations or outings.

    Interests: Adventure sports, nature, outdoor activities, traveling, camping, and thrill-seeking.

    Lifestyle: Active, enjoys challenging physical activities, likely travels for leisure at least once or twice a year.

  • What do they want?

    A thrilling and safe white-water rafting experience.

    Clear information on difficulty levels of the rafting routes.

    Guidance on safety precautions and equipment.

    Package details, if there are packages including other activities or accommodations.

    Transparent pricing and easy booking procedures.

  • What do they expect to find or what questions will be answered by accessing the website?

    Detailed descriptions of the rafting routes and their difficulty levels.

    Safety guidelines and measures taken by the company.

    Pricing details and booking options.

    Customer reviews and experiences.

    FAQs including weight limits, age restrictions, necessary physical conditions, etc.

    Information about instructors/guides and their qualifications.

    Details about the location, how to get there, nearby accommodations, etc.
