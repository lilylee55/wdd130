Down the River

Dive into an unforgettable journey with "Down the River" Whitewater Rafting. Since 1989, we've transformed the raw thrill of the rapids into cherished memories. From the exhilarating splash of the waves to our steadfast commitment to safety and environmental care, we invite you to experience the ultimate river adventure. Join us and become a part of a legacy where every paddle stroke tells a story.

History

In 1989, friends Mia, Jake, and Liam rafted the Colorado River, captivated by its beauty. Inspired, they founded "Down the River" in Riverside, launching tours in 1990. Their focus on thrill, safety, and environment quickly made them a go-to for rafting enthusiasts. As they expanded and ventured into new rivers, they also championed conservation through their 'Rivers Forever' initiative in 2005. By their 25th anniversary, they had hosted over half a million adventurers, staying true to their founding principles of excitement, conservation, and guest satisfaction. Today, "Down the River" invites you to be part of a legacy where each splash is a cherished memory.
