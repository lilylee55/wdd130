Our Trips

Colorado River Rafting

Discover the unparalleled beauty of the Colorado River, with ancient rock formations & towering spires. Enjoy one of the best whitewater rivers. Rafting vacations offer excitement and calm serenity, perfect for families, groups, friends, and solo travelers to escape & harmonize worry-free.

Green River Rafting

Experience the perfect family river trip on the Green River! With fun whitewater, charming campsites, geological wonders, and western history, it's perfect for adventure, quiet time, interaction, and reflection. Strengthen family bonds with lots of playtime!

Dinosaur Rafting Trips

Dinosaur National Monument contains some of the most beautiful and coveted river stretches on the Green River (the Gates of Lodore) and the Yampa River. These trips feature a perfect balance of scenery, whitewater and seclusion. Catch a glimpse of Steamboat Rock while cruising down the river.

Utah River Rafting

Discover Utah's stunning contrasts - from red rock spires to snow-capped mountains. Home to the diverse rafting experiences of the Colorado, Green, and Yampa rivers, Utah offers calm stretches and exciting whitewater, all in one state.

Moab Rafting

Moab is the perfect base for world-class river adventures. Fisher Towers & Cataract Canyon are 30 mins away, Westwater Canyon only 1.5 hrs. Labyrinth Canyon is less than 1 hr. Desolation Canyon is a 45-minute scenic flight. Book yours today!

Specialty Trips

From photography to birding, Sheri Griffith River Expeditions offers a variety of specialty trips led by experts. Gain new skills and memories, with SGRE's professional river guides ensuring an easy and enjoyable experience.
